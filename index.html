Our Story

A little café with a big heart

The Golden Cup was born from a simple dream: a place where neighbours become friends over a perfectly pulled espresso. Founded by Priya and Arjun in 2018, our café sits in the heart of Bandra West, Mumbai.

We source single-origin beans directly from farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur. Every cup is brewed to order — no shortcuts, no compromises. Our kitchen bakes fresh every morning using grandma-approved recipes.
